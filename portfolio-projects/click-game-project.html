Project TwoClick Game

A game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Browser
Github

Users will take care of a virtual pet, similar to the tamagotchi keychains popular in the 90s. The user tries to keep their pet alive until they reach adulthood (10 years old) by making sure they are fed, rested, and adequately played with.

If the user allows their pet hunger, sleep, or play meter reach 10 before your pet grows up, you lose.

Keep your pet alive and well until they are 10 years old and you win!
